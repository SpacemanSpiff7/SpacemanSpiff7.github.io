PIT STOP PUTTING (FRIDAY NIGHT)

Rounds 1-3: Qualifying

  • Closest to the pin, increasing distance each round
  • Worst = fuel penalty. Best = assigns a bonus penalty to anyone.
  • Points: 1st=5, 2nd=4, 3rd=3, 4th=2, 5th=1
  • Ties: sudden-death putt-off, loser takes double

Rounds 4-5: Elimination

  • Round 4: 5 players to 4 (farthest eliminated + penalty)
  • Round 5: 4 to 2 (two eliminated)
  • Final: best of 3, loser takes DOUBLE fuel penalty

Bonus

  • Hole-in-one = everyone ELSE takes a penalty
  • Eliminated players become designated hecklers
