California Seasonal Produce

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the California Seasonal Produce app and related project pages. California Seasonal Produce helps users browse seasonal produce information for California.

1. Information We Collect

The current version of the app is designed to work offline and does not collect personal information from users inside the app.

  • No account creation
  • No analytics SDKs inside the app
  • No advertising SDKs inside the app
  • No location tracking
  • No contact uploads
  • No in-app purchases

2. On-Device App Usage

Searches, filters, and browsing behavior are processed locally on the device to make the app work. Produce and seasonality data are bundled with the app.

3. External Links, Affiliate Links, and Advertising

Future versions of the app or related web pages may include external links, affiliate links, sponsored content, or advertising.

If affiliate links, ads, analytics, or other monetization features are added later, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features go live, and App Store privacy disclosures will be updated as required.

Third-party sites or services may collect information under their own privacy policies once you interact with them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites or services.

4. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data Security

Because the current version of the app does not collect personal information, we do not maintain user personal-data databases for the app at this time.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If material changes are made, the effective date above will be updated.
